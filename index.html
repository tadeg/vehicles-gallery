Our idea

We pretend to be the coolest eClub for vehicle enthusiasts!
Our idea is to connect people who share their passion for vehicles.
And provide the area for exchange of concepts, experiences and fascinations about our passion.

People

We want to connect people who share the same passion and are vehicle enthusiasts. We would like to be creating a virtual place when we can meet each other, make new friends and find soulmates. The place where we can share our vehicle concepts, advices. Our dream is to inspire our club members to new friendships that will expand on our platform and will even move from virtual to real.

Your Vehicles

We pretend to hold an amazing set of information and photos of vehicles of our club members, vehicles that inspire us or our favourite or dream ones. We want to gain knowledge about them, to gain an insight into interesting stories connected with them, to share such snippets. We highly encourage to share images of your vehicles!

Experience

We think it's definitely worth sharing experience, sharing some ideas, some advices for how to move forward with some concepts, for example how to restore an old vintage car. That's what we would love to do - to get together well beyond that stage of having some great ideas - into the stage where you know how to put them into practice. Thanks to shared experience of your soulmates.

Share a Photo

Club's Articles
